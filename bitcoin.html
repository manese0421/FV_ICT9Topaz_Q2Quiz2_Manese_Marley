what is bitcoin?

Bitcoin, introduced by an anonymous user known as Satoshi Nakamoto in 2009, is the first decentralized digital currency. It operates on a peer-to-peer network, enabling users to send and receive transactions without relying on a central authority.

key features of bitcoin

Feature Description
decentralization Bitcoin operates on a decentralized blockchain network that is maintained by users worldwide without the need for central authorities.
limited supply Bitcoin has a capped supply of 21 million coins, which creates scarcity and is a key factor in its value proposition.
blockchain technology A public ledger of all transactions, blockchain ensures transparency, security, and immutability of the Bitcoin network.
